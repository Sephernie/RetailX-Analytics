# KPI Definitions

# Purpose

This document defines the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used throughout the RetailX Analytics project.

Each KPI includes its business purpose, calculation method, required data fields, and where it will be implemented during the project.

These KPIs serve as the foundation for SQL queries, Python analysis, Power BI dashboards, and business recommendations.

---

# KPI 1 — Total Revenue

## Business Purpose

Measures the total income generated from completed sales.

Management uses this KPI to evaluate overall business performance.

### Formula

Total Revenue = SUM(Sales Amount)

### Required Data

- OrderID
- OrderDate
- Quantity
- UnitPrice
- Discount

### Used In

- SQL
- Python
- Power BI

---

# KPI 2 — Total Profit

## Business Purpose

Measures how much profit the company generates after product costs.

### Formula

Profit = SUM((Unit Price − Unit Cost) × Quantity)

### Required Data

- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Unit Cost

### Used In

- SQL
- Python
- Power BI

---

# KPI 3 — Profit Margin

## Business Purpose

Measures profitability as a percentage of revenue.

A higher value indicates healthier business performance.

### Formula

Profit Margin = (Profit / Revenue) × 100

### Required Data

- Revenue
- Profit

### Used In

- SQL
- Power BI

---

# KPI 4 — Total Orders

## Business Purpose

Counts the number of completed customer orders.

### Formula

COUNT(DISTINCT OrderID)

### Required Data

- OrderID

### Used In

- SQL
- Power BI

---

# KPI 5 — Average Order Value (AOV)

## Business Purpose

Measures the average amount spent per order.

Useful for evaluating customer purchasing behavior.

### Formula

Average Order Value = Revenue / Number of Orders

### Required Data

- Revenue
- OrderID

### Used In

- SQL
- Python
- Power BI

---

# KPI 6 — Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

## Business Purpose

Estimates the total revenue generated by each customer throughout their relationship with RetailX.

### Formula

CLV = Total Revenue per Customer

*(Simplified version for this project.)*

### Required Data

- CustomerID
- Revenue

### Used In

- SQL
- Python

# KPI 7 — Repeat Purchase Rate

## Business Purpose

Measures customer loyalty.

### Formula

Repeat Purchase Rate =
Customers with More Than One Order
/
Total Customers

### Required Data

- CustomerID
- OrderID

### Used In

- SQL
- Python
- Power BI

---

# KPI 8 — Monthly Revenue Growth

## Business Purpose

Measures business growth over time.

### Formula

(Current Month Revenue − Previous Month Revenue)
/
Previous Month Revenue × 100

### Required Data

- OrderDate
- Revenue

### Used In

- SQL
- Python
- Power BI

---

# KPI 9 — Top Selling Products

## Business Purpose

Identifies products generating the highest sales volume.

### Formula

SUM(Quantity Sold)

### Required Data

- ProductID
- Quantity

### Used In

- SQL
- Power BI

---

# KPI 10 — Revenue by Category

## Business Purpose

Evaluates which product categories contribute most to company revenue.

### Formula

SUM(Revenue)
GROUP BY Category

### Required Data

- CategoryID
- ProductID
- Revenue

### Used In

- SQL
- Power BI

---

# KPI 11 — Revenue by Region

## Business Purpose

Compares business performance across geographic regions.

### Formula

SUM(Revenue)
GROUP BY Region

### Required Data

- Customer Region
- Revenue

### Used In

- SQL
- Power BI
