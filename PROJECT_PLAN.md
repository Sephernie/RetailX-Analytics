# RetailX Analytics
# Project Plan

---

# 1. Project Overview

RetailX Analytics is a portfolio project that simulates a real-world data analytics engagement for a fictional online retail company called **RetailX**.

The objective of this project is to investigate business performance using transactional retail data, identify trends, and generate actionable insights that support business decision-making.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics lifecycle, including database design, SQL development, Python analysis, statistical modeling, data visualization, and reporting.

---

# 2. Business Scenario

RetailX has experienced inconsistent sales performance over the past several years.

Management has observed fluctuations in revenue, varying customer purchasing behavior, and differences in product performance across categories and regions.

Although large amounts of transactional data have been collected, the company lacks a centralized analytical solution that transforms raw data into actionable business intelligence.

As a Junior Data Scientist, my responsibility is to analyze RetailX's business data and provide evidence-based recommendations that improve decision-making.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

RetailX currently has limited visibility into the factors affecting business performance.

Management needs answers to questions such as:

- Which products drive the most revenue?
- Which customers generate the highest lifetime value?
- What seasonal trends exist?
- Which product categories are underperforming?
- Which regions have the greatest growth opportunities?

Without reliable analytics, strategic decisions are based largely on intuition rather than data.

---

# 4. Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

- Build a normalized relational database
- Perform comprehensive SQL analysis
- Conduct exploratory data analysis using Python
- Create interactive Power BI dashboards
- Identify key business trends
- Provide business recommendations supported by data
- Demonstrate professional analytics workflows

---

# 5. Scope

The project covers the following business areas:

- Sales Performance
- Customer Analytics
- Product Performance
- Geographic Analysis
- Revenue Trends
- Order Behavior
- Customer Segmentation
- Predictive Analytics (Machine Learning)

The project excludes:

- Marketing campaign optimization
- Supply chain optimization
- Financial accounting
- Inventory forecasting (future enhancement)

# 10. Risks and Assumptions

## Risks

- Dataset quality may require additional cleaning.
- Missing values may affect certain analyses.
- Some business questions may not be answerable with available data.

## Assumptions

- Transactional data accurately represents business operations.
- Customer identifiers are unique.
- Product information remains consistent throughout the dataset.
