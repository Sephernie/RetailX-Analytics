# RetailX Analytics
# Business Questions

---

# Purpose

This document defines the key business questions RetailX management expects this analytics project to answer.

These questions guide database design, SQL development, Python analysis, dashboard creation, and business recommendations.

---

# Business Objective 1
## Understand Overall Business Performance

Management wants a clear understanding of how the company is performing over time.

### Questions

- How has total revenue changed over time?
- What is the monthly and yearly sales trend?
- Which months consistently perform best?
- Are sales growing or declining?
- What is the average order value?
- How many orders are placed each month?
- What percentage of revenue comes from repeat customers?

---

# Business Objective 2
## Evaluate Product Performance

Management wants to understand which products contribute most to company success.

### Questions

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which products sell the highest quantity?
- Which products rarely sell?
- Which categories perform best?
- Which categories are declining?
- Which products should be discontinued?
- Which products deserve additional marketing investment?

---

# Business Objective 3
## Understand Customer Behavior

RetailX wants to better understand its customers.

### Questions

- Who are the highest-value customers?
- How much does the average customer spend?
- Which customers purchase most frequently?
- What is the customer lifetime value?
- What percentage of customers make repeat purchases?
- How long is the average time between purchases?
- Which customers are at risk of churning?

---

# Business Objective 4
## Analyze Geographic Performance

RetailX operates across multiple regions and wants to understand geographic trends.

### Questions

- Which countries generate the most revenue?
- Which states generate the highest sales?
- Which cities are growing fastest?
- Which regions have declining sales?
- Are customer purchasing patterns different between regions?

---

# Business Objective 5
## Evaluate Operational Performance

Management wants insight into operational efficiency.

### Questions

- What is the average shipping time?
- Which shipping methods are used most often?
- How many orders are delayed?
- Which products experience the highest return rate?
- Are shipping delays affecting customer purchasing behavior?

---

# Business Objective 6
## Identify Seasonal Trends

Management suspects that purchasing behavior changes throughout the year.

### Questions

- Which months generate the highest revenue?
- Which products sell best during each season?
- Which holidays drive the most sales?
- Are there recurring seasonal patterns?
- Which categories are affected most by seasonality?

---

# Business Objective 7
## Support Strategic Decision Making

Management wants recommendations supported by data.

### Questions

- Which products should receive additional marketing investment?
- Which customer segments should be targeted?
- Which regions have the highest growth potential?
- Which product categories should be expanded?
- What actions could increase overall revenue?
- Which business areas require immediate attention?

---

# Key Performance Indicators

The following KPIs will be used to answer these questions:

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Repeat Purchase Rate
- Sales Growth Rate
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Region
- Top Selling Products
- Customer Retention Rate

---

# Deliverables Supported

The questions in this document will directly drive:

- SQL queries
- Database views
- Python exploratory analysis
- Statistical analysis
- Power BI dashboards
- Executive report
- Final business recommendations

Every analysis performed throughout this project should answer at least one of the business questions listed above.
